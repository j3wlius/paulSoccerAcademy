About Us

Welcome to Pope Paul III Kids Soccer Academy, where we nurture young talent with a passion for soccer. Our academy is dedicated to helping children aged 5 to 17 develop their soccer skills, teamwork, and discipline while having fun.

Located in Rubaga, Kampala, Uganda, we offer professional coaching, modern facilities, and a community-focused enviroment to help your child excel both on and off the field.



At Paul III Kids Soccer Academy, we believe in the power of sports to change lives, and we are committed to guiding every player on their journey to become the best version of themselves.

Our Programs

Junior Program

Ages 5-8. Focuses on developing basic soccer skills, coordination, and teamwork through fun activities and small-sided games.

Intermediate Program

Ages 9-12. Provides more structured training sessions to develop technical skills, game understanding, and physical fitness.

Advanced Program

Ages 13-16. Aimed at preparing young athletes for competitive soccer, including tactical training, endurance, and advanced skill development.

Goalkeeper Program

Specialized training for aspiring goalkeepers, focusing on positioning, reflexes, and decision-making in high-pressure situations.
